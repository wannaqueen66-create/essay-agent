# NOTICE

ScholarLens is a productized adaptation of the open-source `daily-paper-reader` technical base.

The adaptation keeps the web reading shell, GitHub Pages/Actions workflow patterns, Supabase/pgvector storage and retrieval approach, semantic recall, reranking, AI reading, optional Zotero integration, and optional Gist sharing infrastructure. The product domain, crawling configuration, Chinese structured analysis, daily reports, email delivery, and ScholarLens workspace behavior are driven by `essay-agent`.

The original license is preserved in `LICENSE`. User-facing product pages are rebranded as ScholarLens to avoid presenting the upstream project's branding, community promotion, default domains, or service assumptions as part of this product.
